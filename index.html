REIGNSOUND™ NEWS: Ataah King, Black Wall Street Times, Quinn Foster

GLOBAL - Ataah King,¹ rapper for the U.S.-based international music imprint, Reignsound™ Records,² joins forces with TikTok®³ via SoundOn® Global,⁴ TikTok®'s worldwide music distribution arm, for a spin on Ataah's SoundCloud® record Comprehend⁵.⁶

Comprehend (Freestyle)—Remix⁷ is set for release on October 20th, 2024⁸ and clips from the record will be available through CapCut®⁹ and TikTok®'s Commercial Music Library™¹⁰ starting October 4th.

To date, Comprehend, currently streaming on Apple Music®¹¹ and still accessible via SoundCloud®,⁵ is Ataah King's most recent distribution—originally released on December 15th, 2023.¹²

Notably, this date coincided with Ataah's trial regarding claims of assault on law enforcement, which he asserts are wrongful charges.

To learn more, visit The Black Wall Street Times™ for coverage of this incident by Quinn Foster.¹³
